Take It From Us

Let's dismantle whiteness

The concepts we’re talking about are not our original ideas. We are two white guys from Iowa City sharing thoughts we have that have been informed by many different indigenous and black perspectives. The point of this podcast is not to claim expertise about these ideas, but to urge other white people to start (and never stop) deconstructing whiteness and dismantling white supremacy. The title of the podcast is somewhat ironic—you should not take our perception of these concepts at face value. Dig into the resources provided and actively seek out more.
